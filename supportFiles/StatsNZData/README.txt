NZ data sources from https://infoshare.stats.govt.nz 


VSD_DeathsDec_age_sex is from Population : Deaths - VSD : Deaths by age and sex (Annual-Dec) with Time and Observations arrange into the rows section by changing the layout before saving as a csv, to facilitate machine reading 

DPE_PopDecMean_age_sex is from Population : Population Estimates - DPE : Estimated Resident Population by Age and Sex (1991+) (Annual-Dec). Mean Year Ended with Time and Observations arrange into the rows section by changing the layout before saving as a csv, to facilitate machine reading 

DMM_CDRDec_TotPop is from Population : Death Rates - DMM : Crude death rate (Maori and total population) (Annual-Dec) 

DMM_SDR_TotPop is from Population : Death Rates - DMM : Standardised death rates (Maori and total population) (Annual-Dec) 

ITM_2019AgeSEX is from Tourism : International Travel and Migration - ITM : Estimated migration by direction, age group and sex, 12/16-month rule (Annual-Dec) Net with age in rows, sex in columns and only 2019 selected 

ITM_agesex20192020.csv is from Tourism : International Travel and Migration - ITM : Estimated migration by direction, age group and sex, 12/16-month rule (Annual-Dec) Net with age in rows, sex in columns and only 2019 and 2020 selected
